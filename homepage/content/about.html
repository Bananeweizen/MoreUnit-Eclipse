About

The MoreUnit team

The project got published on January 2006 by Vera Henneberger (formerly Wahler). Since May 2006 Channing Walton is a member of the project as well. He had the same idea for the plugin and published SwitchUnit. We merged both plugins and we work together from now on. In 2010 Andreas Groll and Nicolas Demengel joined the project.

If you want to contact us please mail to feedback [AT] moreunit [DOT] org

The MoreUnit blog (development diary) can be reached at moreunit.blogspot.com
